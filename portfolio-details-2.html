ESES (Emergency Situation Escape Simulation)

ESES (Emergency Situation Escape Simulation) is an Augmented Reality (AR) application that demonstrates fire drill with the integration of digital reality. User can feel more real while doing fire drill by using this application.

This AR application can help give more clear vision about the situation that they will face when fire happen. This application visualize the engulfed fire effect, building collapse and explosion. They will be guided from beginning about how to extinguish fire, avoid the fire and obstacles until they success reach the assembly point and their time escape will be recorded.

Built with

  • C#
  • AR Foundation
